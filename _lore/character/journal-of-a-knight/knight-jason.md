Jason is a product of [Project: Colony](_lore/setting/project-colony), raised alongside [Jed Marsh](_lore/character/journal-of-a-knight/marsh-jed.md) and [Sara Lawson](_lore/character/journey-of-a-knight/lawson-sara.md). He was raised among a small family group manning a remote probe ship, and is told that his parents both died during the mission before he could form memories. Arthur, Gwen, Rupert, and Mariah don’t talk about their dead crewmates, so Jason has never known anything about his parents.

The ship arrived in the system of Atrusius when Jason was 7, and spent several months observing the light traffic between the fourth planet and its moons. By the time they approached closer, Jason was 8, and by this age was trained in speaking the Standard English of Project Colony, as well as Mandarin Chinese and Classical Latin, which had been deemed to be the most viable languages for all members of Project Colony to understand in the event they found themselves in a first contact situation.

### Arrival on Atrusius

Rupert and Jason were inserted quietly into a desert during the dead of night, in a desert about two hours walk from the nearest settlement. The shuttle made its way back to orbital distance as they began to make their way to the settlement. Rupert talked the child through their plan, to observe the natives for two days before contacting Arthur with an update and to receive further orders. Rupert would try to figure out the language of the natives with recording equipment, but they wouldn’t be entering the settlement.
