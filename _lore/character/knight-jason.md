# Knight Jason

> [!Personal Information]
> - **Date of Birth:** CE 268X-04-04
> - **Place of Birth:** California, New Gaea
> - **Likes:** Swordplay, Exploration, Leather
> - **Dislikes:** Vehicles, Seafood, Alcohol

Jason Knight was born in the late 27th century and selected by the Government as a candidate for **Project Colony**, an initiative to send colonists to a distant world through a wormhole. It was by coincidence that this wormhole happened to lead to Atrusius. Jason is a hypothetical descendant of the original creator of Atrusius as a setting.

## Project Colony

Jason’s birth parents surrendered him to foster care before his second birthday. He was selected for Project Colony at 3 years old, and assigned to the care of Greg and Emily Jenkins. He became close friends with a handful of other children in the project, and was ultimately chosen as the test subject thanks to his knack for survival skills and languages. At 8 years old, he was able to speak English, French, and Latin thanks to intensive study.

The wormhole had the negative consequence of sending Jason not only to Atrusius, but also sending him roughly 700 years back in time relative to his point of origin. While he was able to communicate with the Government through equipment sent ahead of him, it was also established that the transporter technology could not retrieve anything except communications information.

## Atrusius

Jason was adopted by Darmi Hiro, a recently retired performer, on Atrusius after initiating contact by communicating in Latin. He was able to learn Atrusian International Adinjo, and demonstrated an aptitude for other languages on Atrusius as well. Early on, he seemed to show a remarkable skill at learning everything he studied, though this was later discovered to be because he simply refused to work on anything he felt himself struggling with.

From 16-18, Jason trained under General Belthulk Orgyllas to develop the skills of an adventurer and mage, with specialties in arms and shield combat and in water magic.
