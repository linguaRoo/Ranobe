# Sparks Angela

Angela is a 23-years old Child Development major, and the older sister of [**Gordon Sparks**](_lore/character/sparks-gordon.md). She is a pure hearted woman who is sensitive about her petite figure. She is polyamorous and dating [George Rogers](_lore/character/rogers-george.md), [Josh Bruin](_lore/character/bruin-josh.md), and [Megan Tabor](_lore/character/tabor-megan.md).

After her first session with the [[Refresh Stories/Totema World/Flash! Slaves/Hypnotic Penlight]], Angela lives a double life as [[Namira Slutz]], a whore who replaces Angela at 10 PM every day, after tying up any of Angela’s loose ends. Namira returns to Gordon for her nightly assignments, and slowly begins to replace Angela for longer periods of time. She is shameless about sex, but always requires money up front.

## Flash! Slaves

Angela comes home from her university for summer break. Within a few days, she finds herself fascinated with her now-grown brother, and questions why she’s suddenly attracted to him, and whether this is new or something she’s just been denying. Eventually. Unknown to Angela, she’s been subjected to the penlight while otherwise sleeping, and the seed of the homunculus Namira has already been placed in her mind.

## Lost Light

About two years after [Hypno Slutz](Refresh/Flash!%20Slaves/Hypno%20Slutz.md), Angela is drawn into a sexual underground after being abducted from the apartment where she lives with Megan. After being kidnapped in the middle of the night from Megan’s bed, she wakes up as a prisoner.

Initially kidnapped by her brother, Gordon, she is trained to be his obedient slave and wife, but after finding other familiar faces, she begins to have hope that she can be rid of her abusive sibling for good, if she can only convince one of her friends to save her from Gordon’s grasp…

After eventually assaulting Gordon (when he threatens to breed Megan after he enslaves her), Angela is transferred to the care of Tenshi during Gordon’d convalescence. During this period, she is assigned the name Silk and her roommate, Crystal, works to seduce her at Tenshi’s command.

By the end of the story, Angela has fallen for Crystal, and begs Tenshi to set her free with Angela.
