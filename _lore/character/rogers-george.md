# Rogers George

George Rogers is the first son of Nancy Wagner, and is a freshman at the College. He plans to become a health and fitness trainer, and maintains a leanly muscled form. Despite their age difference, he hit it off quickly [bruin-josh](_lore/character/bruin-josh.md) and [sparks-angela](_lore/character/sparks-angela.md) and became their boyfriend.

Unknown to himself and [wagner-sam](_lore/character/wagner-sam.md), the two are half-brothers. While George doesn’t know Sam very well, he does think that Sam is cute even from the first time they meet. Sam creates the persona of [Chad Jones](Chad%20Jones) in George in order to keep their relationship a secret from everyone, and to assure that George, as Chad, will never question him.
