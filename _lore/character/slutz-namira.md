# Slutz Namira

> [!Personal Information]
> - **Date of Birth:** CE 2024-02-19 (Hypno Slutz, Chapter 1)
> - **Place of Birth:** Glendale, USA
> - **Likes:** Incest, Sexual Corruption, Swallowing
> - **Dislikes:** Prudes, Taboos, Independence

Namira Slutz is a homunculus created in [Angela Sparks](_lore/character/sparks-angela.md) by her younger brother, [Gordon Sparks](_lore/character/sparks-gordon.md). Namira exists to restrain and pervert Angela’s moral standards and psychological aversions, most notably to seeing Gordon sexually and to sex work under Gordon’s management.

Namira is a formerly drug-addicted whore whom Gordon has hypnotized into being addicted to him sexually as a replacement for the drugs she believes she was addicted to. She knows that she lives in Gordon’s sister and is a homunculus, and her greatest turn-on is corrupting and perverting anybody Gordon wants. Namira is a pansexual and will try anything (sexual or otherwise) at least once.

Gordon has promised Namira that once Angela is fully enslaved, he will merge their personalities into a new slave self called Lilith, who will be the queen of his entire harem.

## Lost Light

Namira is initially excited about the return of Gordon to her life, happy to take over piloting Angela’s body and keeping her in line during abusive training sessions. Surprising even herself, however, she comes to discover that she doesn’t _love_ her Master, and has come to love [Megan Tabor](_lore/character/tabor-megan.md). The strength of this connection comes to light when Gordon taunts Angela by threatening to make Megan the mother of his children, and this upsets Namira enough that Angela is able to assault him, with Namira not caring to stop the attack.

Namira has a crisis of identity from this point, knowing she is no longer the perfects slave Gordon made her into, and begins exploring her own sense of self while Crystal is working to seduce her and Angela. Knowing that she needs to grow or die -- and to work with Angela to grow -- Namira falls for the passion Crystal has for darker kinks, and offers to take on the darker side of Angela’s urges.
