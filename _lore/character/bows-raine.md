Raine Bows is a homunculus created in [Skye Taylor](_lore/character/taylor-skye.md) by [Gordon Sparks](_lore/character/sparks-gordon.md) in order to ensure that Skye obeys Gordon even when he is not present to reinforce her hypnotic programming. Gordon sometimes suggests that when Skye fully accepts her place as his slave, Sky and Raine will have become the same person, implying that Raine is a homunculus based on Gordon’s desired “future version” of Skye.

Raine’s primary purpose is to restrain Skye from revealing that Gordon controls her and to keep her limited in how much she can disobey Gordon’s control over her.
