# Sparks Gordon

Gordon Sparks is a 20-years-old pre-law student with a hypnosis kink and a tinkerer’s spirit. He spends his summers making hypnotic toys with his best friend [Sam Wagner](_lore/character/wagner-sam.md), and is shocked when their latest toy, the [Hypnotic Penlight](Refresh%20Stories/Totema%20World/Flash!%20Slaves/Hypnotic%20Penlight.md), works better than he ever intended.

Gordon has had a crush on his sister, [[_lore/character/sparks-angela|Angela]], since he was 14, and makes her his first target. He creates the obedient and sexually needy [[Namira Slutz]] as a persona within Angela so his sister will remain unaware of their incestuous relationship.
