# Kelly, Terra

> [!Personal Information]
> - **Date of Birth:** December 2003
> - **Place of Birth:** Grand Rapids, Michigan
> - **Likes:** BDSM, mind control, nerdification
> - **Dislikes:** Socializing, school cliques, being looked down on
> - **Aliases:** TerraSqueak, TerraMGP

It is unclear how Terra wound up in Alabaster City, but she is a senior at Niemen Technical School, and despite being autistic and having ADHD, she is the top performer of the 2022 (or 2023?) graduating class, even surpassing everyone’s expected valedictorian, Kady Shadow.

When Kady lets Terra know who the valedictorian is going to be, Terra tries to dismiss the importance or significance of it, and then takes Kady’s concern for her as challenge, so she decides to make it clear to Kady that no cat is better than her.
