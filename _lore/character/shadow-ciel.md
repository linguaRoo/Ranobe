# Shadow Ciel Lauren

> [!Personal Information]
> - **Date of Birth:** CE 2006-09-08
> - **Place of Birth:** Lake Earl Commune, Alabaster City, CA, USA, New Gaea
> - **Likes:** Computers, Berries, Psychology
> - **Dislikes:** Violence, Peer Pressure, Housework
> - **Aliases:** Ciel Sonhir, skAI

Ciel is the older twin sister to [Mael](_lore/character/shadow-mael.md), and has a strong incestuous desire to be with her younger twin brother, as well as with the Elvish twins, [Les](_lore/character/oakdell-leslie.md) and [Henri Oakdell](_lore/character/oakdell-henrietta.md). Before the start of *[The Trouble with Twins](Modern%20Stories/Trouble%20Twins/Trouble%20Twins.md)*, she recognizes that she has a paraphilia about twins, and she decides to try and "fix" herself using her experimental mind control technology, the Self-Correction Actualization Interface, but ends up accidentally removing what inhibitions she has regarding incest, and gaining telepathic control over the SCAI nanites.

Her first target is Mael, and after sneaking into his pre-bed shower, she starts working to convince him that as the older twin, she knows best...
