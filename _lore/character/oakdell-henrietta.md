# Oakdell Henrietta

> [!Personal Information]
> - **Date of Birth:** CE 2006-09-07
> - **Place of Birth:** Alabaster City, CA, USA, New Gaea
> - **Likes:** Challenge Sports, Furry Animals, Flirting, Marijuana
> - **Dislikes:** Dresses, Super-sweet Things, Red Meat
> - **Aliases:** Henri (_preferred name_)

Henri is the younger twin sister of [Les](_lore/character/oakdell-leslie.md), and has a little incestuous crush on him. She is also seeing both of the Shadow twins, [Ciel](_lore/character/shadow-ciel.md) and [Mael](_lore/character/shadow-mael.md). Before the start of _[The Trouble with Twins](Modern%20Stories/Trouble%20Twins/Trouble%20Twins.md)_, she is trying to grow up and get over her crush on her brother, until Ciel offers to find a place where they can all be happy together…
