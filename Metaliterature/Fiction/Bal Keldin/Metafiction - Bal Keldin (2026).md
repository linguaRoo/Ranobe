# Bal Keldin – Bal the Superman (Update)

## Bal Keldin

## I Love Bal Keldin!!

Originally a light-hearted series focusing on a reality where the pure-hearted Superman Bal is the most popular man in his Superman academy, _I Love Bal Keldin_ seemed to be following conventions we would associate with a romantic comedy harem series, but began to grow into a supernatural thriller involving demons, undead entities, and other Supermen in the Super Academy.

Initially, Bal is interested in the Superman woman, Liyer, but after his love letter for her winds up in the wrong mailbox, he finds the hot-headed orkish Superman woman, Burna, pursuing him. Every time he tries to clear things up, they just get worse and he draws the attention of the Hol’tiin Riva, the Gramurn Maura, the Kenyu Lakora, and many others, but the more admirers he draws into his orbit, the more rivals challenge him for their affections.

Currently in its second year, the series has begun to incorporate a “Dark World” storyline where ever more creative character designs for new Superman women are competing with each other for Bal, and forcing him to fight off the unwanted attacks of their own prospective suitors.

### Fan Reception

A new generation of fans loves _I Love Bal Keldin!!_, while the older generation often sees it as more of a satire or parody of academic romantic comedies that just uses the Bal Keldin conventions to pander to fans of the Magical Doppelganger and Cyber Soldier stories.

# Enkidu Spin-Offs

## Eternal\~Enkidu Keldin

At the conclusion of _Magical Doppelganger Bal Keldin_, all of the Supermen under Rak’shao are banished to another world, but the unaligned Enkidu finds himself floating in the dark void between realities. This original story explores how he deals with being banished from existence in his original reality and timeline, and travels through various worlds, trying to find a version of Bal with the power and will to restore his existence.

This story is an _unofficial_ expansion of the _Magical Doppelganger_, _Keldin Adventures_, and _Cyber Soldier_ timelines created by a circle of fans who wanted to connect and expand these storylines, notably, the Eternal timeline explains why the Enkidu in _Adventures_ tries to drive Bal into destroying the world, and proposes that the character Luko in the _Cyber Soldier_ stories is not actually an incarnation of Luko (Rather Bal Kieres is posited to be Luko, while Bal or Tempo the Avatar is Bal), but of Enkidu adopting Luko as a false identity.

In the final arc of the story, Enkidu finds Bal Karadu, the founder of the Bal Rebellion, and is able to trick him into freeing Enkidu of his cursed life, giving him a true life in the world of Apokhe. As thanks for his freedom, Enkidu joins his subordinate field commander, Liyer, in leaving the Imperium and joining the Bal Rebellion.

### Fan Reception

Many more traditionalist fans say the series is a convoluted plot without real substance, but for the true Enkidu die-hard fans, this is seen as a way to connect all of the Enkidu storylines into one timeless archetype of the antihero or rogue agent.

## Enkidu Keldin: Lone Wolf

This official miniseries from the author of Magical Doppelganger explores several chapters of the original _Magical Doppelganger_ story from the perspective of Enkidu, showing how he resents the relationship between Luko and Bal, and revealing that he was Luko’s original test subject for the same Superman procedure used on Bal.

The miniseries resolves his story shortly before the final arc of _Magical Doppelganger_ when Luko apologizes to him, and offers to either reverse the Superman procedure or shelter him from the ultimate power. Enkidu says he needs some time to think the offer over, but Luko warns him that there’s no guarantee Enkidu will survive the final strategy against Rak’shao…

### Fan Reception

Most fans of _Magical Doppelganger_ were surprised when this series debuted, but found it quite compelling and satisfying. Especially when they realized that it was the prologue to a brand new story as an explicit sequel to _Magical Doppelganger._

## Lost in Time: Enkidu Keldin

Directly following the miniseries _Lone Wolf_, the same author continued the story in a world that had grown brilliantly advanced in the absence of the Supermen, when Enkidu awakens from a stasis spell.

Enkidu is haunted by the memories of both Luko and Rak’shao as he explores the city, finding that the seeming brilliance on the surface is hiding an oppressed underclass that lives in the underground maintenance tunnels and artificial caverns of society. When he discovers that the underclass is actually composed largely of people who have superman abilities and their families, he takes up the challenge to fight on their behalf…

But when he does, he discovers that the Supermen weren’t absent, only hiding, and that as the most powerful Superman, his once-rival Bal is now a tyrant forcing the peaceful life of the world above. How can he reconcile the admiration he held in his last days before stasis with the mission he now faces? How can he stop a Superman whose powers mirror his own, and with a century of experience in using them?

This series is still ongoing, and in April 2026 it was revealed that Bal has the power to resurrect other supermen, and to pull supermen from other realities, to fight on his behalf. At the end of May 2026, another twist revealed that everybody in this world is a superman: It now appears certain that this is the world Bal created using the Super Splitter at the end of Magical Doppelgänger.

### Fan Response

Many long-time fans cheered this series, and while reactions to the twist of Bal becoming a tyrant were mixed, many see this as the author going back on his former claims that _Magical Doppelganger_ and _Adventures_ are totally separate timelines by telling a story that makes it ever easier for them to bridge the two.

# Liyer Spin-Offs

## Little Witch Lyric

Liyer is a young girl who just wants to hang out with her friends, but when a mysterious creature calling itself a “Familiar” approaches her, she can’t leave it to the mercy of the cruel beasts chasing it. She accepts its hand, repeats the magic chant, “Become ye mine wand,” and transforms into the Little Witch _Lyric._

With the power of her new Familiar, Lyric uses the _Song of Heart_ “Tender Voice” to calm the aggressive beasts and remove evil spirits from them. After the fight, the Familiar introduces itself properly -- Melody the Songstress. Now that they’ve formed a bond, Melody is _Liyer’s_ familiar, and will be by her side, but Liyer needs to protect her secret identity while also seeking out other Familiars and girls who can bond with them.

The series is brand new, and is releasing every two weeks.
