## Enkidu Keldin: Lone Wolf

This official miniseries from the author of Magical Doppelganger explores several chapters of the original _Magical Doppelganger_ story from the perspective of Enkidu, showing how he resents the relationship between Luko and Bal, and revealing that he was Luko’s original test subject for the same Superman procedure used on Bal.

The miniseries resolves his story shortly before the final arc of _Magical Doppelganger_ when Luko apologizes to him, and offers to either reverse the Superman procedure or shelter him from the ultimate power. Enkidu says he needs some time to think the offer over, but Luko warns him that there’s no guarantee Enkidu will survive the final strategy against Rak’shao…

### Fan Reception

Most fans of _Magical Doppelganger_ were surprised when this series debuted, but found it quite compelling and satisfying. Especially when they realized that it was the prologue to a brand new story as an explicit sequel to _Magical Doppelganger._

## Lost in Time: Enkidu Keldin

Directly following the miniseries _Lone Wolf_, the same author continued the story in a world that had grown brilliantly advanced in the absence of the Supermen, when Enkidu awakens from a stasis spell.

Enkidu is haunted by the memories of both Luko and Rak’shao as he explores the city, finding that the seeming brilliance on the surface is hiding an oppressed underclass that lives in the underground maintenance tunnels and artificial caverns of society. When he discovers that the underclass is actually composed largely of people who have superman abilities and their families, he takes up the challenge to fight on their behalf…

But when he does, he discovers that the Supermen weren’t absent, only hiding, and that as the most powerful Superman, his once-rival Bal is now a tyrant forcing the peaceful life of the world above. How can he reconcile the admiration he held in his last days before stasis with the mission he now faces? How can he stop a Superman whose powers mirror his own, and with a century of experience in using them?

This series is still ongoing, and in April 2026 it was revealed that Bal has the power to resurrect other supermen, and to pull supermen from other realities, to fight on his behalf. At the end of May 2026, another twist revealed that everybody in this world is a superman: It now appears certain that this is the world Bal created using the Super Splitter at the end of Magical Doppelgänger.

### Fan Response

Many long-time fans cheered this series, and while reactions to the twist of Bal becoming a tyrant were mixed, many see this as the author going back on his former claims that _Magical Doppelganger_ and _Adventures_ are totally separate timelines by telling a story that makes it ever easier for them to bridge the two.
