# Cyber Soldier: Bal Keldin

> [!Info] Translation Note
> 
> Please note that while our archive includes material from older English translations which localized some Avint names, this source uses our current translation standards and only _notes_ the translations for the names, but uses the original names for all of the characters.

Bahl Kieres (whose name roughly translates to “Rhythm Carpenter”) is a young adult in a public academy (roughly equivalent to American high school) who operates online with the screen name Bal Felorn (“Tempo Sturdy”). He is the captain of the Diveball team for the Stone Valley Gryphons, a local youth sports club. He has a crush on another online operator who uses the screen name Liyr Shoxrom (“Gentl Singing Breeze”), who turns out to be Liyer Firom (“Gentle Dolphin”), an academy student from the nearby Stonebridge Girls’ Academy and the captain of their school Diveball team, the Mares.

At the start of the story, Bahl receives a gift from his father, a piece of upgrade software to convert Bahl’s avatar program from just an interface for the network to an <u>Av</u>atar with <u>Int</u>elligence, or Avint. Over his academy years, Bahl and Bal work together with Liyer and Liyr as well as other friends and allies to defeat an organization called TerrorNet which seeks to take complete control over the network, and through it the whole world.

At the end, the Avint upgrade has become widespread enough that Avints are developing their own society within the network, and the final debates concern whether Avatar society and Human society should be connected or not. After a Tournament storyline, the conclusion reached is that working together in harmony produces stronger humans and avatars.
