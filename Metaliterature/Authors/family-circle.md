The Family Circle is the married couple who publishes under the aliases **Cici Almèn** and **Anak Popo**. They are very protective of their privacy, only revealing that they are a woman and a man who are married to each other (as the profiles above indicate, they do not even reveal their species or age), and while they admit they each produced some stories before they got married under their legal names, they refuse to divulge those stories. They have revealed that they live in Kamelon and enjoy traveling across the entire Millak Peninsula.

They typically publish stories on a fortnightly schedule with about 113 chapter runs, however their recently completed reboot of _Elua the Moon Princess_ was produced on a weekly schedule for 225 chapters, and they used the continuous pace of their publication in _Girls’ Love Life_ to finesse the length of their two consecutive series in that magazine. They have both said that _Elua_ was a fun but challenging project, and that it was a little outside of their comfort zone: Cici said she prefers simple cute girls to the pretty aesthetic Elua called for while Anak said they didn’t have enough time to travel research for other stories due to the high pace.

### Little Lady Shoxani

Shoxani is a 6-year old girl who finds a sandclock (hourglass) pendant that allows her to visit the past and future, bringing anybody connected to her along. She helps her friends learn about history and the principles of cause and effect by using the sandclock, always realizing that the real world is harder to understand than we imagine, and that even if we don’t like the past, it’s not worth changing.

### Check!? The Princess Game!

Maya, a quiet Gramurn girl offers to teach her rowdy Ork neighbor, Pang, how to play chess, but during their game, they wind up drawn into a fantastical chess world where they discover that the White and Red armies are teaming up against a Black army that is invading from above. As a sign of their alliance, the Red and White Princesses—Maya and Pang—can only use their crowns to transform together into Princess Cream and Princess Peppermint. The story is a high action fantasy xáiti that allows both princesses to master all of the moves of their Chess allies, wield weapons based on the needs of the battle, and wear cute armor while kicking tail!

### The Young Lady from the Stars

A Kenyu nobleman whose wife has recently passed of illness is blessed with a daughter from the heavens. The run of this story focuses on her life being raised by the nobleman, her character growth, and what sort of lady she will be when is grown. It roughly covers her childhood, from 5-8, loosely in real time with the run of the series, and ends when she is preparing to enter a private noble Academy. She is often shown to have magical powers, but also a vivid imagination, and that imagination sometimes manifests in heroic or sweet xáiti daydreams.
