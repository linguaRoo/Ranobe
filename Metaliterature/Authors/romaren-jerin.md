# Romaren, Jerin

> [!info] Author Profile
> 
> - **Species:** Adin
> - **Sex & Gender:** Female, Woman 
> - **Pronouns:** _fi_ (she/her)
> - **Age:** 42+ (she is vague on her age, but was at least 12 Atrusian years old when _Pollan Household Stories_ came out, more than 30 years ago)
> - **Nationality:** Veronan
> - **Passions:** Romance, Bal Keldin, Spicy Food
> - **Dispassions:** Simple Stories, Sweets, Drinking Alcohol
> - **Personality Type:** Oralen (looks colorful and sweet, but is actually incredibly spicy)
> - **Affiliations:** Sharp Love Circle (Founding member, A.A. 353), Glitter Heart Romance Circle (A.A. 343-353)

Jerin is a renowned romance comedy author and director who got her start in the Glitter Heart Romance Circle, and debuted as a director of _The Pollan Household Stories,_ directing _Young Lord Pollan and the Maid_ and _The Maid and the Stablehand_ from 346-348. She directed two more stories as a member of the Glitter Heart Romance Circle from 348-353, and finished her responsibilities there while starting her own circle, the Sharp Love Circle, in 353. She typically directs roughly 2-year-long weekly romance stories (150-151 chapters), with a few stories running 3 years (up to 225-226 chapters). She has also made a habit of switching stories at unusual lengths, rather than relying on the length of a contract to shape her story length.

## Biography

Jerin says she grew up in the 330s, reading daiti stories her parents bought for her, but as an insatiable reader she also read her older brothers’ meten and fell in love with Baeron Lassalen’s work on _Bal Keldin_ stories. When she came of age in the 340s, she joined one of the earlier formal circles writing romance fiction, the Glitter Heart Romance Circle, where she apprenticed as an illustrator and inker before pitching the overall idea for _The Pollan Household Stories_ which was approved by the circle and ran from 346-348.

As the second most popular series from the circle at the time, Romaren was entrusted with further leadership opportunities, and used these stories to establish her interest in subversive but genuine romance, largely focusing in the _domyatanu_ genre, with various explorations of the power dynamics of social and financial power. She says this trend is based on her own observations of working relationships with the land-holding class, including a brief period she spent working as a maidservant before apprenticing with the circle.

She began to find her tastes stifled by the strict normality of the _domyatanu_ genre and Glitter Heart’s tendency to flatten the power dynamics she loved to explore, and established her own circle in 353, which has produced 2-3 stories at a time ever since (though she only directs one story at a time, she contributes to her circlemates’ work too). She reports that she loves her work, loves her circlemates, and as she always has, she loves _Bal Keldin_ -- which became very significant when she debuted _I Love Bal Keldin!!_ in 372, which at its onset, seemed like a very traditional high school romance story, and would break her tradition of high power-dynamic subversion.

She says she has had a number of romantic entanglements over the years, but currently states that she is “married to her craft,” and, “the only children \[she] is interested in raising are \[her] stories.”
