---
title: "Whitney's Night Out"
---

“Rattata, Hyper Fang!”  
Nathaniel shouted,  
desperate to make himself heard  
over the sound of Miltank’s mooing.  
“And,  
now!”

The purple rat nodded,  
leapt,  
and struck the final blow to Miltank,  
who collapsed  
as Whitney held out her Pokéball.

“You did good Miltank,  
he just cheated to beat us.”  
she said.  
“Rest up in your Pokéball now.”

With a low moo,  
the cow returned  
in a brilliant  
crimson bolt of light.
