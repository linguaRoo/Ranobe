# 03: First Date

### I - Megan

Megan let out a deep breath. She looked in the mirror and tried to make sure she was looking alright. This was going to be a casual date, a first date, but she was struggling a bit with the idea of dating her best friend -- a girl -- instead of a guy. She knew she was probably overthinking it.
