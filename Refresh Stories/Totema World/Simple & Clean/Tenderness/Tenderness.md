# Tenderness Overview

_Simple & Clean_ is a prequel to [_Flash! Slaves_](Refresh/Flash!%20Slaves/Hypno%20Slutz.md) and [Sacred Bonds](Refresh/Totema%20World/Lost%20Light%20(OCs)/Sacred%20Bonds.md). It is based on an old Digimon fanfiction which shipped the MiyaHika pairing, now using the original characters of [Megan Tabor](_lore/character/tabor-megan.md) and [Angela Sparks](_lore/character/sparks-angela.md). Unlike the original _Tenderness_, this story will also feature [George Rogers](_lore/character/rogers-george.md) and [Josh Bruin](_lore/character/bruin-josh.md), setting up the polyamorous core relationship the four have in _Flash! Slaves_ and _Sacred Bonds_.

In addition to the specific focus on allowing the _Girls’ Side_ and _Boys’ Side_ stories to be told, there will also be sections (after about the middle of the book) in which other relationships are explored.

At this time, _Simple & Clean_ is a codename. The story should receive a new title before the first chapter is published. The story will be published online for free, on AO3, and possibly on FurAffinity, and SoFurry. It will not contain any explicit sex scenes, though because it explores queer relationships we expect there may be some backlash.

## Outline

Megan is frustrated with boys who only want sex. She vents her frustrations to her friend Angela, who asks if Megan has considered girls instead. Over several months, they develop a closer relationship together.

In the meantime, George and Josh start to bond over their passion for sports and Angela -- and discover that their willingness to share with each other is stirring something unexpected, culminating in a surprising moment of intimacy.

Angela realizes that as much as she does love Megan, she can’t make herself love _only_ Megan, and that she still wants to try dating a boy. Megan admits that she feels the same. Unaware of the boys’ relationship, the two ask George and Josh out separately. With better communication, they do learn that the boys are together as well, and eventually, all four agree to try a polyamorous relationship.

## Conflicts

The primary conflicts explored in this story are the emotional challenges of exploring a same-sex relationship, with bits of social pressure against it as well. In the second half, the struggles of being polyamorous are also explored. There is more external conflict as people suspect the couples are cheating once they start dating across the establishing couplings.

## Contents

The story will contain heterosexual, lesbian, and gay male couples, as well as polyamory in a negotiated tetrad polycule dynamic. This is to say that it will include scenes of physical and emotional intimacy up to and including petting, snuggling, hugging, hand-holding, kissing, and sleeping in the same bed. It will include scenes depicting internalized homophobia, possessive behavior, jealousy, discomfort, and other such emotional content.

It will also contain scenes of joy, pleasure, comfort, and other positive emotional content. It is part of the politics of this book to show the start of what will become a strong, stable, supportive and happy polyamorous relationship.
