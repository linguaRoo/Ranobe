# _Flash! Slaves_ Overview

Hypno Slutz is a mind control story inspired by a previous fanfiction story, but with all fanfiction elements removed, and all characters presented being 18+.

[Gordon Sparks](_lore/character/sparks-gordon.md) (20) and [Sam Wagner](_lore/character/wagner-sam.md) (20) develop a [Hypnotic Penlight](Refresh%20Stories/Totema%20World/Flash!%20Slaves/Hypnotic%20Penlight.md), which allows them to quickly induce deep trance in others, and they test it out on [Angela Sparks](_lore/character/sparks-angela.md) (23) and [George Rogers](_lore/character/rogers-george.md) (23). After successful testing, each of the men attempts to build their own harem out of their peers.

When [Megan Tabor](_lore/character/tabor-megan.md) (24) begins looking into Angela’s strange behaviour, Gordon reveals he’s not interested in nerds, but does give a spare Hypnotic Penlight to her to give to her boyfriend [Kevin Prima](_lore/character/Prima-kevin) (24) -- a gambit to keep Megan out of his own affairs that pays off.

While Sam is happy to just take a few boys (George, [Josh Bruin](_lore/character/bruin-josh.md) (23), and [Conrad Green](_lore/character/green-conrad.md) (21), with occasional trysts with Gordon under the pretense of “sharing notes”) once in a while, Gordon becomes obsessed with complete control over each of the subjects he takes after learning that they hate his control (he takes several other unnamed women during the story as well, experimenting on them) -- he uses their trances to make joyous “homunculi” in them, a forcefully split personality that is obsessed with worshiping and serving him, something tied to them in a way that would be difficult to remove, and makes them incapable of revealing his control over them.

Eventually, Skye is able to communicate her distress to Sam (as he knows about Gordon’s control, she can talk to him about it). Sam is genuinely disturbed to realize how far Gordon has gone, especially when it’s revealed he’s apparently used the penlight on [a cat](_lore/character/myau_silk) for similar sexual purposes. She is also able to express her distress to a [mysterious stunning blond-haired man](_lore/character/seraph-tenshi.md) (even though she can’t reveal her actual problem to him) who is looking for a lost cat fitting the unusual description of Gordon’s enslaved cat…

With the help of Sam and the mysterious man, Skye is able to overcome Gordon, confiscating his penlight, and setting things right. They confiscate Gordon’s Hypnotic Penlights, removing what suggestions from his victims (Angela, Skye, “Silk”, and a selection of other women) they also confront Kevin, and once he’s dealt with, and Megan is freed from his influence. They then confiscate all of the penlights, with Sam admitting that it was too dangerous to leave any of them around.

In the epilogue, it’s revealed that charges were filed against Gordon, and he has disappeared. The effects of the story on Angela, Megan, Josh, and George are outlined, with a final note that Skye has also disappeared, though she left a letter for Angela saying she would be back when she felt like she had really worked through everything that the experience had done to her.
