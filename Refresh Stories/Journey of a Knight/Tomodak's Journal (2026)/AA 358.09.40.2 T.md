# Tomodak’s Journal (2026 Edition)

## 2. Security

The morning was uneventful. I did buy a second spool of silver thread, and gathered the materials I need to keep our wards maintained. I’m no priest, but I can channel enough power to keep the sacred wards operating each night. The silver thread doesn’t need any charging, but does need to be kept above the ground. The slightest amount of dirt will break the ring.

For this dry run, we’ll all be in one tent, though we’ll have a second for Sara while we’re on the road. Jésen is setting it up. Guarn played the mediator, too. We brought the rations for this test run, and Guarn swore that he’d fix us a meal once camp was settled. But we’ll be taking about a week of rations each on the road. Enough to be safe, but not enough to slow us down.

We spent about 3 hours getting out here. Not a trivial walk home, but not so far that we can’t signal if we need to. I’ve been assigned to gather firewood, and then I’ll be setting up the silver thread perimeter and other wards. I have enough stakes to give us a good 10 yards radius, and to keep it taut.

Guarn will be doing the survival side. Jason is doing the tent. I’ll go do my part now.
