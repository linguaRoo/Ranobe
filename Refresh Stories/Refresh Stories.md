# Stories

## Totema World

Totema world is a [brand safe](https://tvtropes.org/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/SerialNumbersFiledOff) reinterpretation of old Digimon Adventure fic. The primary storyline for this setting begins with the story [Tenderness](Refresh%20Stories/Totema%20World/Simple%20&%20Clean/Tenderness/Tenderness.md), then is followed up by [Flash! Slaves](Refresh/Flash!%20Slaves/Hypno%20Slutz.md), and continues into [Sacred Bonds](Refresh/Totema%20World/Lost%20Light%20(OCs)/Sacred%20Bonds.md). As a series, this ties the ideas of old Digimon fanfic into an original world.

### **(WT) Simple & Clean Trilogy**

#### [Tenderness](Refresh%20Stories/Totema%20World/Simple%20&%20Clean/Tenderness/Tenderness.md)

This story explores the development of romantic-sexual relationships between [Angela Sparks](_lore/character/sparks-angela.md) and [Megan Tabor](_lore/character/tabor-megan.md). 

#### (WT) Confidence

This story explores the development of romantic-sexual relationships between [Josh Bruin](_lore/character/bruin-josh.md) and [George Rogers](_lore/character/rogers-george.md).

#### (WT) Sharing

This story follows _Tenderness_ and _(WT) Confidence_, and explores the two couples coming together into a polyamorous arrangement.

### **(WT) Flash! Slaves**

A few years after _(WT) Sharing_, Angela and George’s younger brothers develop a [hypnotic penlight](Refresh%20Stories/Totema%20World/Flash!%20Slaves/Hypnotic%20Penlight.md) which allows them to control anybody -- and they start out by sexualizing their older siblings and friends.

#### _Flash!_ and Release

[Sam Wagner](_lore/character/wagner-sam.md) develops the [hypnotic penlight](Refresh%20Stories/Totema%20World/Flash!%20Slaves/Hypnotic%20Penlight.md) and tests it out on [Gordon Sparks](_lore/character/sparks-gordon.md). After its success, he shares a spare with Gordon, and uses his for homosexual catch-and-release style play until [Skye Taylor](_lore/character/taylor-skye.md) comes to him begging for his help and unable to explain what she needs help with.

#### _Flash!_ and Cover

Gordon uses his hypnotic penlight to start playing with his older sister, Angela, and making her work as a prostitute named [Namira Slutz](_lore/character/slutz-namira.md). He also impulsively tries the penlight on a feline-like Totema who sees him conditioning Namira, and finally goes after his other crush, Skye Taylor, who remains resistant to his suggestions despite the power of the penlight.

#### _Flash!_ of Lightning

After the events of the previous books, Sam teams up with Skye and a new angelic Totema (who takes on the name Tenshi) to take Gordon’s penlight from him, and to undo the damage he’s done to Angela, Skye, and the feline Totema he named Satine. Gordon escapes them at the last minute, but loses his penlight in the process.

### **(WT) Sacred Bonds**

A few years after _Flash! Slaves_, Gordon returns and kidnaps Angela from her bed -- after using his newly made penlight to disable Megan. Planning to brainwash her into always being Namira, he holds her as his prisoner in the APERTURE facility, where he runs into his old nemesis, Tenshi, and sets his eyes on vengeance too -- wanting to claim both Satine and Skye (and, in fact, everything) from the angel.

#### (WT) Fallen Angela

Angela is kidnapped and kept aware of Namira living her life as an imprisoned slave. Tenshi and Skye discover that Gordon is in their home base, and has kidnapped Angela.

#### (WT) Angela’s Seduction

Gordon assigns the blue-haired slave he acquired from the APERTURE management to seduce and corrupt Angela through an elaborate vampire roleplay. Tenshi struggles with the bureaucracy of APERTURE to protect his harem from Gordon.

#### (WT) Divine Gifts

Tenshi is finally free to move after Gordon poaches Skye, and he does so in force. However, he is only authorized to retrieve Skye -- so how can he save Angela, the Vampire, and several other slaves Gordon has acquired in his latest assault? After being freed, Skye gives herself to Tenshi again, worrying Angela…
