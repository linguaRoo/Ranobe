# The Refresh Project

The Refresh Project is a 2020s project to update old fanfic writing as original stories. While character relationships from the original sources (both the original IP and the old fanfic) are likely to be preserved, the characters will be reinterpreted as original characters with new names, and details of the settings may be changed.

Original Characters from the old fanfic may be preserved but updated, or reinterpreted like the official characters used in them.

This is not being done for the sake of making money. While it does _enable_ sale of these stories in a commercial marketplace, the reasons to write a Refresh story is the feeling that the story deserves a second chance to be explored, not just that I want to sell it. Refresh stories are derivative works from the original fanfiction, rather than completely new stories exploring the same ideas.
