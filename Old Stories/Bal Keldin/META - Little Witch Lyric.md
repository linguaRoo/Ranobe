# Keldin Girl - Little Witch Liyer

The most recent Keldin series to enter publication is _Mornai Keldin—SHOXECHIN Xáiti’tal_, formally translated as _Keldin Girl—Little Witch Liyer_ and localized into English as _Little Witch Liyer_. It is written by a private husband and wife team and is being published on a fortnightly schedule in the anthology _Everybody Read!!_, an all ages mixed-gender meten anthology magazine. _Everybody Read!!_ is published weekly on Tuesday (every _romjia_ and _eljia_ on Atrusius).

The husband and wife team working on the project publish under the aliases _Cici Almen_ (translating into English as “Sweet Cream”) and _Anak Popo_ (“Never a Papa”). They are known for being very good at writing and illustrating cute girls of all sorts, having primarily worked on other _xáiti’tal_ series.

## Broad Summary

Liyer is a young girl who just wants to hang out with her friends, but when a mysterious creature calling itself a “Familiar” approaches her, she can’t leave it to the mercy of the cruel beasts chasing it. She accepts its hand, repeats the magic chant, “Become ye mine wand,” and transforms into the Little Witch _Lyric._

With the power of her new Familiar, Lyric uses the _Song of Heart_ “Tender Voice” to calm the aggressive beasts and remove evil spirits from them. After the fight, the Familiar introduces itself properly -- Melody the Songstress. Now that they’ve formed a bond, Melody is _Liyer’s_ familiar, and will be by her side, but Liyer needs to protect her secret identity while also seeking out other Familiars and girls who can bond with them.

## Chapter Summaries

### Chapter 1

(published 2026-Feb-24)

The story is set up with Liyer, an 8 (Atrusian) years-old girl[^3] taking care of animals at her mother’s veterinary practice before her afternoon session at school. As she makes her way back to school, she sees a mysterious spotted cat-like animal grooming itself on a wall. She meets back up with her friends Relya and Tari as they all head back to school. The chapter ends with a dark figure sending out two dark spirits with a simple command: “Capture Qonshoxem.”[^2]

Liyer parts ways with Relya and Tari on her way home, and is approached by the cat-like creature from earlier. It introduces itself as a Familiar (in Adinjo _réjolav_ “family spirit”) and asks Liyer to help protect it. As soon as the Familiar makes the request, two stray dogs appear chasing after it -- Liyer scoops the Familiar up and promises to protect it at her mother’s clinic.

### Chapter 2

(published 2026-Mar-10)

The stray dogs seem to grow larger and work together to corner Liyer and the Familiar in an alleyway. The Familiar nuzzles Liyer’s palm and tells her to chant the words, “Become ye mine wand.” When she does as instructed, the Familiar transforms into a silver wand with the Familiar’s face, and pink spinels for eyes -- and Liyer herself is transformed into an armored school uniform, and declares herself “Little Witch Lyric” (in Adinjo _SHOXECHIN Xáiti’tal_ “LYRIC the Little Magic Girl”).

Able to feel the disharmony within the stray dogs, Lyric fights defensively until the Familiar’s voice in her heart tells her to sing the song of love in her heart. Using the Tender Voice song, two dark spirits are driven out of the stray dogs, and Lyric uses her wand to purify the spirits. The purified spirits take the form of fireflies and merge with her wand. She reverts out of her Little Witch form and the Familiar thanks her for assistance, then asks Liyer to look after the stray dogs, who were hurt by the fighting.

### Chapter 3

(published 2026-Mar-24)

Liyer brings the stray dogs to the clinic and after telling her mom that she found them limping, she goes upstairs. The Familiar enters her bedroom through a window and admits that she is married to Liyer now. Her name is Qonshoxem, or Melody, and she has the power to sense and heal the hearts of other living beings. She has a mission to find and purify corrupted Familiars, and Liyer must help her now that they are married.

Liyer pauses at the repeated use of the word “married.” She admits that she hates to see animals or people suffering, but isn’t sure she can take on the weight of being Lyric so easily… until she hears a scream from downstairs. Rushing downstairs, she finds that one of the birds downstairs has grown almost as big as herself and her mother has passed out.

### Chapter 4

(published 2026-Apr-7)

The chapter opens with Liyer reaching out to Melody, chanting the incantation, and transforming. The bird lets out a piercing screech and grabs Liyer’s mom in its claw before bursting through the wall and hopping up to the neighborhood roofs. Lyric chases it, and Qonshoxem tells her that this bird is not possessed by a spirit, but a corrupted familiar. If they can learn its name, they can bind and purify it, but the Tender Voice will be useless.

The fight against the pet bird crosses the rooftops of the whole neighborhood, with Lyric singing to release sound wave attacks and the bird countering with bursts of wind to disrupt the sounds. Qonshoxem eventually says she’s figured out the spirit’s name: Arrozom.[^3] If Lyric can use the Song of Heart “Calling Home” with that name, they can draw Arrozom out of the bird. Lyric protests the idea of using this immediately -- her mother and the bird would be hurt if they call Arrozom out while it’s flying.

### Chapter 5

(published 2026-Apr-21)

Lyric repeats that she can’t put her mother or the bird in any more danger by trying to stop the familiar Arrozom. Qonshoxem is understanding and helps her try to lure the bird back to the ground. They are able to get it to the ground in a park, using the bird’s need for food to force Arrozom to the ground. As it eats suet, Lyric chants the Calling Home and draws out the sparrow-like familiar Arrozom. As the bird shrinks back to its normal size, Lyric uses the Tender Voice to purify the Familiar, who thanks her and offers to support Qonshoxem.

Lyric reverts to Liyer and wakes her mother up. Jenais, her mother, says she remembers seeing a shadow out of the corner of her eyes, then got attacked by a giant bird. She doesn’t remember what happened until she woke up, but she’s happy that Liyer is okay. When they return home, she is upset about the damage to her ground floor clinic, but happy to see that apparently none of the animals escaped. Up in Liyer’s room, Qonshoxem and Arrozom tell her that she can only be married to one Familiar, so Arrozom needs the protection of another person as soon as possible. The chapter ends with Relya drawing a picture of Lyric fighting the giant bird.
