# Meta-X: Conflicted Hearts

## Prologue:

One year ago, Nathaniel began manifesting strange powers, and discovered similar powers in his friend Julia within the past six months. Unaware that Melvin Grayson, the new transfer student to his class, also has similar powers, and that his best friend Jason has them too, Nathaniel rains in secret to cope with these powers while others his age are coping with nothing worse than acne and girl problems.

But all of that is about to change, as a new element enters his life this year, 2001, just after he finishes elementary school. He finally says goodbye to these early days of his life, leaving behind the teachers that he’s known forever, only to be thrust into a local adventure that nobody is expecting…

# Meta-X: Year One

---

June 8, 2001: Nathaniel Knight

Dear Diary,

This is my last day of school coming up, and then it’s two and a half months of freedom. Man, I can’t believe that Julia has the same kind of powers I do, even if they are really, well, totally different. She’s willing to test them even, but I think she’s still more concerned with being a dancer than anything else. Oh well…

Darn, it’s time for me to go. I’ll finish this up tonight, with how the day went, though. Later, diary.

! Nathaniel !
