_Mistress doesn’t waste time when she needs to get things done. Geneva seemed bothered as I entered the room, because her lesson plan had been disturbed by Pandora’s orders. Of course, this was when I learned that not all slaves are created equal, and not just in the sense of senior or junior slaves._

_Trainers have authority over their curriculum, and the students in their care. This authority includes a responsibility for failures (I learned this a bit later) and the freedom to negotiate curriculum changes with the Owners. A lot of this I picked up from hints instead of direct questioning, but Trainers within their classes are a a class of slaves all their own, and a lot of them have leadership tendencies to begin with. It’s quite common for a Trainer and Owner, even their own Owner, to come into conflict when lessons plans are changed from either end._
