## Background

Written by Austin Springer in 1999, during their sophomore year of high school, ages 15-16. This is the foundational story establishing the world of Atrusius, though the earliest versions of Atrusius are barely recognizable in the modern age.

Atrusius is a hybrid of science-fiction and fantasy, with 28th century human Jason Knight sent to Atrusius from Earth through a wormhole-based teleportation device by an Earth Government organization called “Project: Colony.”
