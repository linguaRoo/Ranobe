Sarah was very pleased with herself. She’d already gotten Melanie under once, and this would be almost two weeks of progress if it worked. In a single day. Plus, Melanie really seemed to like Dave, so if she could just nudge those two together the plan would work even better.

“Melanie, can I demonstrate hypnosis on you before we start our game?” Sarah purred the words, careful not to use the trigger that would make Melanie accept it as her own idea this time. “Just for a moment so the boys can see how natural and easy it is.”

Melanie looked across the coffee table and picked up her already-empty coffee mug as if she was thinking. When she set it back down, after almost half a minute, she nodded. “Sure. But how can you demonstrate it?”

“Earlier, when I helped you with yoru focus, I gave you a special keyphrase that helps you go into trance easily,” Sarah explained, puntuating with a sip of her coffee. “I’d like to use that to show the boys how easy it can be to drift into trance, but then I want to wake you up so we al play the same game we did before.”

Melanie blushed. “I don’t remember that part,” she said. “But, I guess that means I don’t think it’s important, right?”

Sarah nodded and set her mug down. “I’d like to show them Melanie’s myth.”

Melanie’s eyes fluttered and her arms slipped down. Sarah watched almost in slow motion as the trigger brought Melanie not just into a trance, but the deep trance she’d managed to reach earlier. The loosening of her muscles and tension, the lolling of her head. Her jaw going slack and her tongue just barely staying inside her lips.

“Melanie, how do you feel?”

“Calm,” Melanie answered. “Relaxed.”

“Very good,” Sarah said. “Now in a moment, when I snap my fingers and say ‘wake’ you will wake up remembering everything about this trance except the words I used to bring it about. You know I have a command to make you relax, but you can forget what that command is. Do you understand?”

Melanie nodded slowly for a few seconds before she said, “Yes. Easy to forget.”

“That’s right. Now wake,” Sarah snapped her fingers as she said the command, and smiled.

Melanie’s eyes fluttered open, and she let out a calm breath. As she started to adjust her arms and legs into a more polite pose, she looked at the boys, then Sarah. “Wow. You are a really great hypnotist,” Melanie said. “I’m not sure what you said, but… I was out like a light. Are you sure it isn’t magic?”

The boys laughed a bit at that. Sarah just smiled. “I’d dare you boys to try and resist me,” she said with a smile. “But Melanie was bigger challenge than I expect either of you will be.” That should help her a lot. Build up her image by suggesting Melanie was hard to hypnotize. Of course, there was a little truth to it. Sarah did tend to find it much easier to hypnotize boys than girls, but the fact was that she had a lot of practice with boys and knew many ways to get them distracted enough early on that their attention could be captured properly by her words.

“Alright,” she said, looking between the two of them. “The rules are simple. I will say something. If you agree with it, just repeat it. As you repeat, you’ll find you’re focused on my words and deciding whether you agree or not, and that will help you drift into trance. Later on, I may add some more rules to help you go deeper once you’re already in a light trance.”

And so the game began. Easy things, both agreeable and not, nothing to challenge them. After about fifteen minutes, she finished the game, telling them to just relax and follow her guidance. She decided to use a stairway deepener to help keep things more stable across her three subjects, and since her only real goal was Melanie’s trust… she didn’t intend to take Greg or Dave for herself. Making them too focused on her would be a flaw in her plan.

Finally, she was sure they were all quite calm and relaxed. She gave them a trance command, one they would all share until they went to sleep tonight, and then woke them up for a moment.

“So, I hope you al enjoyed that, and see how easy it is for me to take control,” Sarah said, purring the words out as she looked to them. “Tonight, I’d like to let each of you choose something sexy you want to do, but not actually having sex. Maybe you’d like to touch one of the other people here and see how their body feels and responds, or maybe there’s something you just want to do that feels sexy. I promise that in the morning, you and I will be the only ones who really remember it, and I won’t make you feel bad about what you want.”

After a few seconds of deliberate silence, she continued. “Once you make a choice, feel a balloon tied to your wrist lifting your hand up, pulling you back into a light trance. Once all of your arms go up, I’ll drop you all back to a deep trance, okay?”

Melanie swallowed and nodded. It looked like she was already decided, as her wrist started to lift within a few seconds. Greg took a little bit longer, and David took the longest, almost thirty seconds before his arm started to lift up. “Very good. You can still change your mind as your hand rises, if there’s something you’d like even more than what you first chose, but when you’re certain, the balloon pulls harder, raising your hand as you drift down into trance.”

Sarah watched as Melanie’s head lolled and her hand rose to the point Sarah almost thought it might pull her out of her seat. It was so hot to watch the responses, the differences in how they reacted. Greg’s eyes fluttered as his wrist cross the level of his gaze, which seemed to be fixed on Sarah. Dave’s eyes closed early on, but his hand rose quickly. Despite the delay in starting, his arm was upright very quickly, before Greg’s was even starting to bring his arms straight.

When Greg’s arm snapped into position, Sarah smiled. She’d always worked her subjects one at a time before, but this was going to be very fun, her first time hypnotizing three people at once with different plans for each of them. She licked her lips and then said the trigger to bring them down.
