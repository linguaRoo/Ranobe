title: 2.01e - Sarah
---

Sarah stopped. There was already a clue in there. Amber was Aphrodite. Dio was a very poorly disguised Dionysus. Harvey was a male, so she was guessing either Hermes or Hades. Still, neither of them was actually associated with medicine, at least not directly. Asclepius was the god of medicine, and he wasn’t one of the big ones most people remembered.

“Hello, miss Sarah? Angel’s Wings to miss Sarah?” Harvey was trying to get her attention. She smiled and nodded. “Like I was saying, it’s nice to meet you, and if you have any medical needs, don’t hesitate to call for me.”

“Of course,” she replied. “Thank you for introducing yourself, Mr. Harvey. I’ll remember that.”

He gave a polite bow. “Then if you’ll be so kind as to excuse me, I just want to check in with the other one before I head back to my office. Take care, miss Sarah.”

Sarah paused and mirrored the bow. “You too, Mr. Harvey.”
