## Outdated

“Welcome home, slave,” Terra said softly. “I know it’s been a long time since you were here, so I’ll show you around.”

Kadelya found herself dragged inside, with the door hastily slammed, and locked, behind her. The garage had apparently been converted into an apartment, with drywall and a ladder leading up to the loft, where mistress probably kept her computer and bed. The floor was linoleum, and looked a bit damaged, as if it had been moved into the garage whenever mistress Terra had made it an apartment.

“Oooh, still in your uniform. No, that won’t do for my slave. Blazer off at home, Kady. Shoes off, too. Obey.”

Kady didn’t hesistate, unbuttoning her blazer and slipping it off, then stepping out of her own shoes. “Like this, mistress?”

“Good girl,” Terra said. “I don’t really do guests much, but I have a lot of fun toys I’ve been working on here ever since I put you undercover. Text your family, tell them you’re spending the night with me.”

Kadelya didn’t think, she just grabbed her phone and write a text to her dad. \<Terra asked me to stay the night, love you dad.> As soon as she hit send, she saw Terra holding out a hand. She gave her mistress the phone.

“You won’t be seeing anybody except me for the night, slave. Let’s restore my nerdy girlfriend and integrate her with the agent who’s been seducing Sophie for me.”
