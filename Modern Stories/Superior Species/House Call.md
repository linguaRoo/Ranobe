## House Call (Kadelya)

Kadelya took a deep breath as she approached the old garage. It was in one of the denser neighborhoods of Alabaster, and the houses were more run-down than they were by the coast. She double checked the address that her mistress had texted her, and it was the right house, the garage matched the picture. Her heart was almost dancing in her chest as she walked up the driveway and stopped at the garage. There was a raising door for a car, but on the left there was also a regular door, and it had a bright yellow and black sign reading “Private Property,” with a black and white sign under it, “No Trespassing.”

She knocked at the door. There was a clatter from inside, followed by a stream of expletives muffled by the door. It took a moment for the blinds on the side of the door to get pulled back, and then for the door to open.

“Hey, so it’s a heck of a mess in here,” Terra said, her voice a little bit husky. “I… don’t really have anybody over. If that’s a problem for you, then you can, y’know, fuck off.”

“Or you could visit my place,” Kadelya said, tilting her head to the right. “But I guess my Mistress would prefer the home field advantage, right?”

Terra rolled her eyes and opened the door. “Yeah, whatever, Class Pres. You live at the freaky sex cult compound, guess I shouldn’t be surprised you’d throw that out so easily.”

Kadelya shrugged and stepped inside past the mouse, her brow furrowing as she set her suitcase and purse down on the garage floor. “How to put this… I don’t like when people call my family a cult. There’s no real belief behind the commune. Dad says it was incorporated, legally without any religious background, when my big sister was adopted.”

“Whatever, that’s just what people call it,” Terra said, closing the door. “Look, I don’t do this much, but I did try to research this whole sleepover thing, so we can talk and shit as much as you want. Since you offered to let me hypnotize you…”

“After you started without my consent,” Kadelya said, holding up one finger on her left hand. “But if you researched you should know the first thing a host does is show her guest around, right?”

“I was getting to that,” Terra said, grumbling as she tried to get back onto an apparent script she’d set up. “I just wanted to say that since you’re gonna let me practice my kink on you, maybe we should get to know each other. A bit. So… Now I’m ready to show you around, _princess._”

Kadelya grinned a bit as Terra turned around. She never got to sub for Sophie. Mark was only really into it because she was, and he wasn’t a very good dom apart from his confidence. And nobody else was around who would play with her this way. “Got this place all to yourself, huh?”

“Yeah, after I blew up a laptop battery in the house, my uncle let me take over the garage,” Terra said. “So downstairs, here, is my lab. Whatever interests me, as long as I don’t blow the garage up. Which is pretty much everything robotic, computers, and y’know, mind control.” Terra seemed to shudder a bit at the last. “Which I wouldn’t tell anyone except you, catslave.”

Kadelya smiled. Terra was opening up to her. That was exactly according to plan, letting Terra do something intimate with her like hypnosis should give them a chance to get closer. Even if it wasn’t romantic, or even if it was just transactional, Kadelya wasn’t really looking for another _girlfriend._ But if it happened, having a girlfriend as a mistress would be convenient and hot.

Terra walked past workbenches full of cool-liking devices in various states of assembly and disassembly, not really pointing any of her experiments out. “Don’t touch my things without permission,” she said. She stopped by a ladder. “Apartment is up the ladder from inside, but the bathroom is down here.” She pointed off to the right.

Kadelya nodded slowly. She had noticed the garage floor was just a slab of concrete, with some rubber mats thrown down in what she assumed were major walkways. “So you pretty much live on your own here?”

“Uncle has to pay the bills and keep me fed until I graduate,” Terra said, starting to pull herself up the ladder. “Set your shit back behind ladder. I made an elevator.”

Kadelya nodded and walked around the wall the ladder was fixed to. There was an old shipping palette with some plywood nailed on top of it and cables at each corner. By the simplest definition, it was an elevator. She put her suitcase on top of it, and her purse, then walked back around to climb up the ladder.

She glanced up and saw the ladder was clear. Curses! Terra had outsmarted her desire to peek up the mouse’s skirt by distracting the submissive-feeling cat with the elevator. “I’m coming up,” she said, raising her voice enough to be clearly heard.

She started to climb when she felt the wall begin to vibrate. Terra must have started the elevator. She wondered if the apartment would be as messy as the workshop floor and in her musings felt her head bang against the ceiling.

“Oww! Why is the ladder…”

“Elevator safety,” Terra said firmly through the wood panel. “The ladder lets out onto the elevator floor or roof, so the ladder door needs to be closed when the elevator is running.” The tone she used made it sound so obvious, and maybe it was, but Kadelya hadn’t thought about that. Then again, as a vet, she didn’t always think about mechanics much…

“That makes sense,” she muttered. The vibrations stopped and Kadelya heard four metallic KER-CHUNK sounds. After a short wait, the door at the top of the ladder opened to Kadelya’s left, and Terra grunted as Kadelya saw her securing a latch up to the wall. “Sorry, I didn’t think about the arrangement,” she said as she pushed herself up the top of the ladder, her claw tips digging slightly into the wood of the elevator floor for grip.

Terra huffed and puffed more like a wolf than a mouse, and Kadelya couldn’t help but grin. “So, mistress, why don’t you teach your catslave how to operate this for you?”

“Wh-what? What are you smirking at? Do I need to spank you for being insolent, you bitch?” Terra’s voice got tight as she spoke, husky again in a way that just made Kadelya want to purr, even though she was enjoying teasing the mouse. “Or is that what you’re after? Are you a masochist who wants me to spank her?”

“Pretty sure I’m not a masochist,” Kadelya said, unable to suppress her giggling. “But I am a brat. Or at least, I’m trying it on. I’m a switch, and all of my partners right now are submissive.”

Terra blinked a few times. “Oh? Don’t you have a lot of people in your commune or whatever?”

Kadelya took a deep breath. “Yeah, but they aren’t fucking pedos,” Kadelya said. “And because I grew up around them, it doesn’t matter how hot the adults are, I see them all like parents and uncles and aunts. So nobody there can really play with me, even if I wanted to play with them. Come on, that should be as obvious as the layout of the ladder and elevator, right?”
