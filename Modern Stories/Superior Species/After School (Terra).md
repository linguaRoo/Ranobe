## After School (Terra)

Terra was ready. As most of the teens were heading home or to a room for their clubs, she stood by the broad window of the atrium, directly across from the main entrance. She knew enough about Kadelya that she was confident her prey wouldn’t pussy out, she was a goody two-shoes like that.

But a small part of her was still surprised to see the catgirl with her fox girlfriend, approaching Terra. Before she could hear them over the rush of other students, she saw Kadelya lick the foxgirl’s cheek and then part ways with her, the fox carrying two bookbags, and Kady only bringing a water bottle with her. Terra tried to remember who the foxgirl was. Brown? She wasn’t important. Kadelya was the one who had challenged her.

As Kadelya got close, Terra offered her hand for a handshake. She took a deep breath as her rival responded in kind. She had to be quick, especially with the crowd. As their hands clasped, Terra gave a sudden tug and wrapped her free arm around the catgirl. “Sleep. Quiet. Obey. Follow me, Shadow. Follow me to the lecture hall, silent as can be.”

She felt the cat’s body go limp for a second, felt the shock, but she had apparently done it right. After a brief tense shock, Kadelya was staring a bit blankly, and nodded at Terra. She didn’t say a word.

“Right. Third floor, north wing,” Terra said, letting go of Kadelya. “Nod if you understand.” She slowly let go of Kadelya’s hand, watching for the response.

Kadelya nodded.
