## Weak Point (Terra)

Terra held Kadelya firmly by the jaw, no longer pulling the slave in for a possessive, claiming kiss, but holding her in place, shocked by what the catgirl had told her.

“Say that again, princess?”

Kadelya almost purred this time as she spoke, her face growing flush with what might be embarrassment. “I am weak to hypnosis, mistress.”

What exactly did that mean? Was she easy to hypnotize? Well, obviously, she’d been a wonderful subject in the lecture hall, and the library, throughout the week. Not that their library sessions had been kinky, Terra wasn’t ready to press that rule yet, though she knew it would be the first to test Kadelya on. “Explain that to me,” she said firmly.

“My hypnotic studies involved reading, watching, and listening to a lot of scripts,” Kadelya said as her tail started to swish behind her. “While daddy allowed me to do the research, he also helped keep me safe. But in the course of the research, I learned that my mind is weak to hypnosis. I accept suggestions easily. I forget them slowly. I spent a weekend stuck as a goth slut in October, though Sophie kind of enjoyed that…”

“Stuck as?” Terra’s eyes were cocked, and she was fascinated with this idea. “You mean you couldn’t be suggested to get out of it?”

Kadelya nodded slowly. “The recordings took me very deep into trance. They made the goth slut persona very deeply rooted, and resistant to removal. Daddy used some sort of… it’s hard to even explain it. But he was able to get me back to normal by Sunday night so I could be myself for school on Monday.”

Terra could feel her mouth watering. “So you were voluntarily enslaving yourself to these other hypnotists just to study it? What kind of weak pushover are you to fall so easily?”

“The kind that is weak to hypnosis,” Kadelya said, smiling softly over the deepest blush. “The kind that wants a dominant who can exploit that weakness and make… make your pleasure my bliss.”

Terra let out a long, slow breath. She looked Kadelya over. “You… I don’t ever want you to lie to me. I know how easy it is to lie to people, so I want an oath of honesty from you, princess. And not just your word, because frankly I don’t trust anybody. But start with your word. Swear on your hypnokink that you will never lie to me.”

The cat’s breath caught in her throat as Terra watched. “Of course. I swear on everything that turns me on that I will always be completely honest with you. I swear on my weakness to hypnosis that if I lie to you… may I drop into trance immediately.”

“Is that all it takes?” Terra admired the image, but didn’t believe it could be that easy to drop her new slave. “Or do I need to actually make that oath real for you?” she held out a hand and pointed straight at the floor.

“Well, it wouldn’t hurt,” Kadelya said softly, but clearly. She moved to kneel on the floor, looking at Terra with a surprising cheerful glint in her expression. “But maybe?”
