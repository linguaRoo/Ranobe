## Safety and Compromise (Kadelya)

Kadelya blinked several times. She already knew she was in a decent trance. She was comfortable. But Terra hadn’t actually asked her if this was okay. She reached out in her mind, and gave the slightest tug on a suggestion she knew she’d given herself. It was easy to go along with the trance, and Terra hadn’t done anything too bad yet. But it could get bad if she didn’t do something right now.

She knew that she was very suggestible. It was already feeling very easy to focus on Terra. Like she would forget what was happening if she kept listening longer. It felt so very _very_ tempting to give in to the idea of forgetting and accepting. But she needed to be smart and safe. She needed to choose this for herself.

“Caffeine.” Her eyes fluttered softly, and she felt herself become less focused, more aware.

Terra’s eyes were getting narrower. She seemed surprised, taken aback, maybe not quite shocked, but definitely upset. “What the hecky? What are you doing?”

“I’m going to talk to you, Terra.” Kadelya smiled a soft, demure, toothless smile, a sign that she wasn’t trying to threaten her classmate. She looked up at the mousegirl in front of her. “The first thing I want to tell you is that I’m having fun. I never would have thought you were into this sort of thing, but I enjoy hypnosis too.”

Terra nodded slowly. She didn’t seem to have expected this reaction and seemed to be trying to keep her guard up, as if Kadelya might turn the tables and try to force her own rizz on the mouse. It was kind of cute, and if she had permission, Kadelya wouldn’t hesitate. After a moment of concerned silence, Terra sat on the teacher’s desk. “What are you planning, Shadow?”

“I’m planning to let you drop me again,” Kadelya said, feeling her smile grow. “I’ll let you try whatever you like… But I have three conditions.”

Terra squirmed a bit on the desk, her maryjane shoe pressing hard against the floor. “What is this, some sort of genie wish bullcrap?”

Kady raised a hand to her face and brushed a few stray strands of hair out of the way. She chuckled softly as she chose her words. “No, I’m not giving you three wishes. Just the three rules. Rule one, my safe word is caffeine. I do not drink or consume caffeine, so if I say or write the word, or if I signal with three taps, that means we stop. Is that acceptable and clearly understood?”

Terra looked away from Kadelya for a moment. At the whiteboard, the back of the lecture hall, the door she’d placed the desk against. Then she nodded. “Fine. I guess you’re smarter than I expected, so I’ll respect your hecking safe word. What else?”

Kadelya felt her tail swishing, playing along would be a great way to show Terra that she was trustworthy, and to get Terra to open up, maybe. “I’m agreeing to play with you, and you’re choosing to play with me. There is no passthrough. You might already be thinking about it. How do I know enough about hypnotism to wake myself up? Who else do I play with? That’s my concern, not yours, even if you want to be my Mistress.” She felt the blush rising in her cheeks and stammered out, “N-Not that I’m committing to that level yet!”

Terra’s eyes moved like she was reading something. The blush. The stammer. Of course, Kadelya had no reason to expect _Terra_ had anybody for her to get at -- but she knew that if she didn’t lay out the no passthrough rule, Sophie would be at risk. Not just Sophie, Mark too. She had a responsibility to keep her partners safe. Terra didn’t have to worry about that, she guessed. After all, if she did, she might have softened up a bit over the years.

“Oh, you think you hold all the cards here,” Terra said, growling between and through the words. Her teeth chattered a bit at the end, too. “Fine, you’re the one I want to mess with anyway. Everybody else leaves me alone like I want, and I leave them alone. I won’t pry into your other shit. What’s the third rule?”

Kadelya took a deep breath. She wasn’t sure if she entirely believe Terra, but she chose to trust her. The third rule… All three rules were equally important, except that the first was slightly more so than the other two. As much as Kadelya was into the hypnokink, as much as her own girlfriend was a nympho, if even Sophie had agreed to this rule, then she was sure Terra could do the same.

“The third rule is propriety.” She let out a firm breath through her nose. “No sex at school. I am perfectly fine if you want to use your power over me for sex. I’m not even a virgin, but… it would be a shame if the two smartest girls in school got expelled before graduation for being inappropriate in public.” She paused and flashed a little grin. “Plus, mommy and daddy might not let me visit you if we got caught. Those three rules and… I’ll let you hypnotize me. You can try whatever you want within those rules.”
