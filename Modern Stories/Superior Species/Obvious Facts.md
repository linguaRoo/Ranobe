## Obvious Facts (Terra)

Of course that was obvious. The freaky sex cult had standards, and tried not to abuse their kids, huh?

“Yeah, I guess so,” Terra said, snorting. “Alright, fine. So, uh, finish the tour. I mean, not that it’s too tricky. The main door is in the back, past the elevator, into the kitchen. I don’t cook much except ramen and instant frozen shit.” She started walking around the elevator.

Kadelya rocked and bounced herself up, grabbing the outer cable and swinging around it to face the kitchen. It wasn’t much, but it had a vinyl flooring that offered decent grip for pads and socks, the cabinets were all some damaged parts that had been dirt cheap, and the only real fixture was the sink, though it had no waste disposal, so Terra had to keep a mesh in the drain to keep it from clogging. Kadelya scurried into the kitchen even as Terra watched. “So what do you drink? I don’t see a water filter on the tap,”

“Vanilla Coke. And I can’t handle too much caffeine myself, so I get decaf and add my own vanilla,” she said. “Tried some other flavors, but vanilla is the only one I care for, help yourself if you like the lime or orange syrups. And for water, I have a filter attached out back. Tap’s clean.”

She walked over to the full-sized fridge and opened it up. It mostly had sandwich fixings, salad mix and dressings, and what even she considered an ungodly amount of soda cans, all with the red and dirty brass color scheme that Coke used for their caffeine free offering. “Catch,” she said, tossing one to Kadelya. Not that she was much for aim, but the cat had the sort of reflexes one would expect of her species, and caught it easily enough. As the fridge closed, Terra set her own can on the counter and opened the upper cabinet nearest the fridge, and got out a wire rack with three flavor syrups and room for a fourth. She cracked open the can and added one pump of vanilla to her can then swished it around gently.

“So that’s the kitchen.” Terra turned to Kadelya, who was opening her own can of cola. Terra grunted. “So, it’s all a studio layout, from the garage door the kitchen here is back-left, my library and school computer are back-right, and the front is my living space and bedroom. I don’t really split those up too much.” She turned and gestured toward each room as she mentioned it, then started walking up front. “Any questions?”

Kadelya took a sip of the soda and narrowed her eyes. “Mmm…” she was swishing the syrupy liquid around her mouth like it was her first time ever drinking a Coke. “Is it weird to say this is too sweet? Oh, questions for you, you mean?”

Terra snorted at that. “Have you never had a Coke before?”

“Nobody has it caff-free,” Kadelya said with a shrug. “And we make our own soda at home, daddy says trying to order for everybody’s taste would be too much trouble. Oh! I have a question for you, mistress.” She paused for a second, then added, “If I may?”

Terra nodded. “Go ahead, slave. What’s your question?”

“Do you plan to fuck me tonight?”

Terra’s eyes went wide at the absolute bluntness of the question. How should she even answer that? Of course, they were both old enough, but she didn’t have near enough control over Kadelya to do that yet, but if Kadelya _wanted_ to do it, did Terra need to rely on control? She still wanted it, obviously, but…

“I think for tonight…” She swallowed deeply. “Fuck. Look, I never expected this shit to happen. I wanted to hypnotize you and make you obey me, yeah, maybe fuck you, but mostly make you humiliate yourself by making you a fucking nerd who would cream herself if she got a wedgie or called a dyke. But then you kinda blew that idea up by knowing what was going on.”

Kadelya frowned at that. She took another sip from the can, her muzzle scrunching up at the taste she’d called ‘too sweet.’ Terra was worried she had upset the cat. “How did me consenting to this blow… oh. Oh! You wanted to do it without my consent! But… you know you can’t just do that sort of thing in real life, right? It’s not like the stories.”

Terra let out a deep sigh. “Oh yeah, well it was…” She paused herself. Of course she _knew_ the legal acceptability of it. And what most people would think of it ethically. “You’re right. But I’m messed up like that. And impulsive. So…” she hated what she was about to say, but knew it was necessary. “Sorry I dropped you without asking. Thanks for not turning me in.”

There was an extended silence as Kadelya walked to the living room area. As she stood next to Terra, she reached a hand out to touch the mouse’s. “I wouldn’t turn in my first chance at a proper domme,” she said softly. “But, yeah, doing it without asking was kind of rude. But you did a good job, and when I did realize it enough… you agreed to do it better.”

Terra nodded. Slightly. “Yeah, whatever. So, I guess if you’re gonna let me do this, I should get to know you a bit. You told me a bit about what you know the other day… but I want to know more.” It was obvious this was the right way to approach this. Even if Terra didn’t care about keeping everything “right” as she continued, she needed Kadelya to trust her after the fuck-up in the lecture hall. “So… You have partners. You said you’re a switch and they’re subs, right? Do you hypnotize them?”

“Of course,” Kadelya said, stepping onto the futon mattress laid out on the floor. “Oooh, fair’s fair, mistress. Can I ask you question too? To get to know my mistress better?”

“Fine, but… I reserve the right to not answer.”

Kadelya giggled, the slight tittering sound setting Terra’s nerves on edge. “Oh… well how about we make it more fun. You want to fuck me, I want you to fuck me. So how about making a stripping game out of it? If we refuse to answer, we take off one piece of clothing.”

Terra rolled her eyes. “Hmm?” She really hated when other people gave her rules. But this could be fine. “Fine. Ask your question.”

“You were more socialable when you moved here in 4th grade,” Kadelya started, holding up her left hand with her thumb folded in. “And we were in the same class that year too. So, why did you transfer in?”

This was Kadelya playing her little friendly game? This question was where she wanted to start? To get closer to Terra? “I’m not going to answer that right now,” Terra said, taking a deep breath. She kicked at her left heel with her right foot, loosening the sneaker. Then she did the same for the other, rolling her eyes. When both were loosened, she shook them off and held out her stocking-clad feet, the thigh-high striped socks shaking in front of Kadelya’s face. “There, shoes off. My turn. What’s the most fucked up thing about your life?”
