## Schoolyard (Terra)

“Do you even know her name, mistress?”

Terra paused. “Of course I know Brown’s name,” Terra said. “And you know I just said it right, so there’s no reason to avoid saying it now, slave.”

“You’re right. I’m sorry, Mistress, I just thought you didn’t know Sophie’s name.”

“Well I do. Sophie Brown is the one who always looks uncomfortable even though she makes whatever she wears looks better than anybody else in this school. So I repeat my question, slave: Would you give Sophie Brown to me?”

“I’m happy to give you anything you want, mistress,” Kadelya answered. “Even my girlfriend is yours if you want her.”

“Good.” They reached the main doors of the school and found the principal waiting to lock up. “Hey, you should give me your phone number, Kadelya. I guess I misjudged you.”

Kadelya recited off her phone number, and Terra entered it into her phone as they stood outside by the public bus stop. She stepped in closer to Kadelya after she had saved it, and gave the catgirl an awkward hug. “Forget about giving me your phone number, slave. You forgot to exchange numbers with me, but you only realize it when you get home.”

Kadelya gently hugged Terra in return. “Hmm? Oh, it was nice… getting to know you, mistress. Guess it’s time to get home now, huh?”

“That it is. I actually need the bus on the other side of road,” Terra said. She dared to try implying intimacy before she left though, and nervously licked Kadelya’s cheek, the way Kadelya had done with her girlfriend earlier. Kadelya returned the gesture, then let go of Terra. “Have a good night, slave.”

Kadelya’s cheeks were glowing as Terra started across the street.
